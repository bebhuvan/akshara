================================================================================
DESIGN PHILOSOPHY
================================================================================

1. READING FIRST
   Every design decision serves the reader and the text.

2. MOBILE IS PRIMARY
   India is mobile-first market. 44px touch targets. Optimized for 3G.

3. SPEED AS FEATURE
   <50KB initial load. ~15KB JavaScript. Fast builds. Fast deploys.

4. WARMTH OVER COLDNESS
   Paper tones (#fffffe), not stark white. Cozy, not clinical.

5. NO BORDERS
   Depth through backgrounds and spacing. No boxes or rules everywhere.

6. CHARACTER WITHOUT CLICHÉS
   Sophisticated, not generic. Indian, not stereotyped. Editorial quality.
