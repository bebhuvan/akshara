Preserving Indian literature for future generations

Akshara is a digital archive dedicated to preserving and sharing public domain works of Indian literature. We believe these texts deserve exceptional reading experiences, not just functional digitization.

Our Mission

Between 1847 and 1954, Indian writers, philosophers, and activists produced extraordinary works that shaped the nation's intellectual and political landscape. Many of these texts exist only in poorly scanned PDFs, making them difficult to discover and painful to read.

We're changing that. Every book in Akshara is meticulously digitized from historical sources, manually corrected for accuracy, and presented with reading experiences designed for extended engagement—not quick skimming.

This is more than preservation. It's about making these works genuinely accessible to contemporary readers while honoring the care their authors put into the original texts.

Quality Over Quantity

We digitize works we can do justice to, not everything we can find. Each book receives individual attention.

Reading First

Every design decision prioritizes the reading experience. Typography, spacing, navigation—all serve the text.

Open Access

All works are public domain. All our digitized texts are freely available in multiple formats.

Historical Accuracy

We preserve original spelling, punctuation, and formatting choices while correcting obvious OCR errors.

How We Work

01

Source Selection

We find the best available scans from Archive.org, university libraries, and institutional collections. Quality of the source material matters.

02

OCR Processing

PDFs are processed using Tesseract or Google Cloud Vision API to extract text. This is just the beginning—OCR alone is never enough.

03

Manual Correction

Every page is manually reviewed. We correct OCR errors, verify quotations, and ensure formatting matches the original.

04

Markdown Formatting

Clean text is formatted in Markdown with proper chapter breaks, emphasis, and structure preserved from the original.

05

Metadata Research

We add historical context, biographical information, and reference links to enhance understanding without overwhelming the text.

06

Publication

Books are published with EPUB exports, downloadable Markdown, and links to original sources. Nothing is hidden or proprietary.
